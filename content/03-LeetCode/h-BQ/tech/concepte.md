**HashMap vs TreeMap (in English)**

### What they are
- **HashMap**: stores key–value pairs in a **hash table**.
- **TreeMap**: stores key–value pairs in a **balanced binary search tree** (typically a Red-Black Tree), so keys are **kept sorted**.
